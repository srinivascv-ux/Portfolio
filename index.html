What I believe

The lens that
finds everything.

Every concept on this page came from the same question — asked in a different place.

01
Design ships before it is finished
The most dangerous design failures are not the ones caught in testing. They are the ones that make it all the way through engineering, approval, and into the hands of real people — where they quietly cause damage nobody is measuring.
02
The body is always the brief
A rider tilts their head. A plant operator stays alert at 3am. An office worker stares into a white screen for 8 hours. The human body is the real specification. Everything else is a design decision made by someone who forgot that.
03
White space has nothing to do with white
Breathing room between elements. The pause between thoughts. It can be black, textured, deep navy — any colour at all. If it carries no information, it is white space. The industry has been saying this wrong for 40 years.
